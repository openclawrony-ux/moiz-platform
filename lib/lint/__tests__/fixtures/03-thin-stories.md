# Internal tool PRD

## Problem

Our analytics team manually exports CSVs from three dashboards every Monday. The job takes 90 minutes and is error-prone.

## Target user

Analytics team leads (4 people).

## User stories

- The tool exports the three dashboards on a schedule.
- The tool emails the CSVs to a shared inbox.

## Non-goals

- Not replacing the dashboards themselves.

## Success metric

Weekly time saved from 90 minutes to under 5 minutes within one month.
